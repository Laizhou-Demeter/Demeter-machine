About Demeter

Demeter is a professional manufacturer integrating R&D, production, and sales of industrial compression molding equipment. With years of expertise in the composite materials machinery sector, our core products include SMC/BMC sheet molding compound production lines, four-column hydraulic presses, and high-precision compression molds, delivering one-stop solutions from material preparation to finished part molding for customers worldwide.

Equipped with advanced CNC machining centers and a rigorous quality management system, our products are exported to Southeast Asia, the Middle East, South America, and beyond, serving the automotive components, electrical & power, building materials, and municipal infrastructure industries.
