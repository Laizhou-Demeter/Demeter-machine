Demeter Exhibits at CAMX — The Composites and Advanced Materials Expo 2025
2025 · USA

Laizhou Demeter Imports And Exports CO., LTD made a strong impression at CAMX 2025 (The Composites and Advanced Materials Expo), one of the largest and most influential composites industry events in North America. Held annually in the United States, CAMX brings together manufacturers, suppliers, distributors, and end-users from across the global composites supply chain.

At the exhibition, Demeter presented its latest SMC/BMC sheet molding compound production line technology, four-column hydraulic press solutions, and high-precision compression mold capabilities. The booth featured detailed technical displays, product sample demonstrations, and live consultation sessions, drawing steady foot traffic from industry professionals, procurement managers, and technical engineers throughout the event.

"CAMX 2025 was an excellent platform for us to connect with the North American market directly," said the Demeter exhibition team. "We received very positive feedback on our equipment's build quality, automation level, and competitive pricing. Several promising cooperation agreements were initiated during the show, and we look forward to developing these relationships in the months ahead."

Demeter remains committed to expanding its global footprint and will continue to participate in leading international exhibitions to bring its compression molding solutions to more customers worldwide.

Demeter Successfully Concludes Participation at GEC 2024
2024 · Global

GEC 2024 (Global Equipment & Composites Exhibition) brought together leading manufacturers and technology providers from the international composites and industrial equipment sectors. Demeter participated as an exhibitor, presenting its comprehensive range of compression molding equipment to a diverse audience of industry professionals.

Throughout the exhibition, the Demeter team engaged in productive technical discussions with composites experts, raw material suppliers, and equipment buyers from multiple countries. Key topics included SMC/BMC formulation optimization, hydraulic press automation trends, and mold design for high-volume production. The exchange of ideas and market insights proved invaluable for both Demeter and its visiting partners.

"GEC 2024 exceeded our expectations in terms of visitor quality and business opportunities," commented the Demeter management team. "The face-to-face interactions allowed us to better understand the specific needs of different markets and demonstrate how our customized solutions can address them. We left the exhibition with a strengthened network and clear direction for future product development."

As part of its ongoing international strategy, Demeter plans to continue participating in major global exhibitions, reinforcing its position as a trusted partner in the compression molding equipment industry.
