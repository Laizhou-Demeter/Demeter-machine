Who We Are

Laizhou Demeter Imports And Exports CO., LTD is a specialized manufacturer integrating R&D, production, and sales of industrial compression molding equipment. With deep technical expertise in the composite materials machinery sector, we focus on three core product lines: SMC/BMC sheet molding compound production lines, four-column hydraulic presses, and high-precision compression molds.

From material preparation to finished part molding, Demeter delivers complete turnkey solutions tailored to each client's production requirements. Our engineering team brings years of hands-on experience in automation, hydraulic servo control, and mold design, ensuring every machine meets rigorous performance and reliability standards.

Quality First — Every machine undergoes strict factory acceptance testing before shipment, backed by ISO-certified quality management.
Innovation Driven — Continuous R&D investment in servo-hydraulic technology, intelligent control systems, and energy-efficient designs.
Customer Centric — From initial consultation to after-sales support, we partner with clients to maximize production uptime and product quality.
